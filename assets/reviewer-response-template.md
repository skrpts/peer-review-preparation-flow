**Formatting notes:**
- Always paste the reviewer's exact comment in blockquotes — do not paraphrase
- Number comments sequentially within each reviewer's section (1.1, 1.2, ... 2.1, 2.2, ...)
- Provide specific page and line numbers for all changes in the revised manuscript
- Maintain a respectful, professional tone throughout
- If a comment requires no change, always provide a justification — never leave it blank
